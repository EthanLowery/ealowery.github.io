FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this website for? (Alternative) What's the website's purpose?

The website is a portfolio of Ethan Lowery, and it's main specific purpose is to show the author's
work that may appeal to gamers, enthusiasts, or anyone looking for a game dev to collaborate with.



Who is the author?

My name is Ethan Lowery, I've majored in Computer Programming and Network Security at Wake Tech Community College.
More information about my education, experience, and skills can be found in the resume linked below.



What made the author inspired enough to pursue game development?

I always love the idea of creating my own world and immersive experience that would engage the player through captivating visuals,
solid gameplay. That was my personal experience with games and I want to share that with others.



How do you make your games?

I use visual studio code mostly. The mark-up and scripting languages like HTML to lay foundation, css for creating simple shapes and design pieces, and Javascript for interactivity.




Does this website enable visitors to download the creator's games?

Yes, but only the demos are available to the public for free.
To gain access to the full version of the games here would require payment.
